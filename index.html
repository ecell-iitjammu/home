About Us

B2B Talk Show

Back to Basics Show is a fresh, fun and authentic look at the common people who achieved their goals and gained success. We will pull back the curtain on how academics and co-curricular activities really helped the alumini in business and jobs and how they are really faring in the current marketplace. Our goal is to bring you every time engaging, entertaining and actionable content that will arm you with the information and insights that will guide you as a business owner, future business owner or investor that wants to win in the world of business.
